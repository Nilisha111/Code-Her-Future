About Us

Code-Her-Future is a nonprofit organization on a mission to empower women to break into the tech industry-with confidence, skills and, community. We provide hands-on coding education, mentorship, and career support to women from all walks of life, especially those who are underrepresented in tech. Because we believe that when women are given the tools to succeed, they don't just change their lives- they change the future.

Programs

  • Code Camps

    Fun, hands-on workshops and camps for girls to explore coding and tech.

  • Curriculum Lab

    A creative, student-focused coding and computer science curriculum.

  • Mentor Connect

    Supportive mentorship from women in tech to guide and inspire.

  • Create and Code

    Project-based coding sessions where girls build real apps and games.

  • Tech Talks

    Short career spotlights from women leaders in the tech industry.

  • Learning Hub

    An ongoing resource center with coding challenges and tutorials.
